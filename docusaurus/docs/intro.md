# Introduction to Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

Welcome to the comprehensive educational resource for AI Systems in the Physical World. This book covers embodied intelligence, humanoid robotics, and the integration of artificial intelligence with physical systems.

This book is organized into six main parts:

- **Part I**: Introduction to Physical AI
- **Part II**: The Robotic Nervous System
- **Part III**: Simulation and Modeling
- **Part IV**: Advanced Perception and Planning
- **Part V**: Humanoid Control and Interaction
- **Part VI**: AI Integration and Autonomy

Use the navigation menu to explore the various chapters and topics covered in this comprehensive guide to humanoid robotics and physical AI systems.
