# Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics - Book Cover Design Document

## Project Overview
Design a professional, modern book cover for "Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics" with cutting-edge aesthetics suitable for an advanced AI/robotics textbook.

## Visual Specifications

### Color Palette
- **Primary Background Gradient:** `#0A1F44` to `#1B3B6F` (dark blue gradient)
- **Purple Accents:** `#8B5CF6` (primary purple), `#A78BFA` (lighter purple)
- **Text Colors:** White (`#FFFFFF`) and light blue (`#E0F2FE`)
- **Accent Highlights:** Glowing effects using purples and blues

### Typography
- **Main Title:** Large, bold, modern sans-serif font
- **Subtitle:** Medium weight sans-serif, smaller than main title
- **Bottom Text:** Clean, readable sans-serif font

## Layout Structure (1600x2400px)

### Top Section (Upper Third)
- Main title: "Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics"
- Bold typography with subtle glow effect
- Positioned prominently in upper third

### Middle Section (Center)
- Central visual element: Silhouette/wireframe of humanoid robot
- Circuit patterns and neural network connections
- Holographic effects with purple/blue lighting
- Movement and intelligence conveyed through pose and effects

### Bottom Section
- Subtitle: "Embodied Intelligence for the Future"
- Author information or "AI-Powered Interactive Book"
- Feature icons: Interactive content, RAG chatbot, Personalization

## Design Elements

### Background
- Dark blue gradient background
- Abstract geometric patterns
- Subtle grid lines or technical diagrams
- Floating data points and connection nodes
- Layered depth effects

### Glassmorphism Elements
- Translucent panels with backdrop blur
- Subtle transparency effects
- Modern glass-like surfaces

### Central Robot Visual
- Wireframe or silhouette humanoid figure
- Glowing circuit patterns overlay
- Neural network connection lines
- Dynamic pose suggesting movement
- Holographic projection effects

### Lighting Effects
- Purple and blue glow effects
- Subtle neon lighting
- Gradient overlays for depth
- Particle effects for high-tech feel

## Mood and Atmosphere
- Cutting-edge technology
- Intelligent and authoritative
- Modern and accessible
- Inspiring and forward-looking
- Professional academic quality

## Technical Requirements
- Dimensions: 1600x2400px
- High contrast for readability
- Professional appearance
- Suitable for both light and dark UI themes
- Scalable vector elements where possible
